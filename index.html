About BiteLocal 🍔

We made BiteLocal for the FBLA Coding & Programming 2025-2026 competition. The topic this year is "Byte-Sized Business Boost" which is about helping people discover and support small local businesses. We chose to make a restaurant finder app because we thought it would actually be useful for students and families in our community.

BiteLocal lets you browse local restaurants, filter by food type, search by name, read and leave reviews, save your favorites, find special deals, and even submit restaurants we're missing. Everything works and there are no errors!

How We Built It

Language: Python 3 — we chose Python because we learned it in CS class and it's readable

Framework: Flask — lets us make multiple pages and handle forms

Templates: Jinja2 — so we don't copy the header/footer on every page (DRY principle)

Styling: CSS3 — we wrote all our own styles

Fonts: Google Fonts — Bebas Neue and Nunito

Images: Unsplash (free to use)
